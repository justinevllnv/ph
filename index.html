About

PHassiSTI is an online assisting tool for STI Academic Center Caloocan's different school transactions that still uses paper-based forms. Our group as well as our Project adviser came up to this solution because of every enrollment period's mahirap na transaction between students and their respective Department Heads. The registrar, as well as the IT supervisors could not accommodate so much students in a period of time...
